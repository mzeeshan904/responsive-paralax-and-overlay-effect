Oopen

Our first Wallnuts stairway mural! Ent Design was doing a complete interior design for the new Utrecht office of Oopen, and she asked us to help out with the stairway. The colours are based on the interior palette and the illustrations are inspired by all the different types of companies Oopen works with.
